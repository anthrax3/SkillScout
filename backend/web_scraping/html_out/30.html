About Us

At Cloudflare, we have our eyes set on an ambitious goal: to help build a better Internet. Today, Cloudflare runs one of the world’s largest distributed networks that powers more than 1.5 trillion pageviews each month across 5 million Internet properties. More than 10 percent of all global Internet requests flow through Cloudflare’s network. Cloudflare protects and accelerates any Internet application online without adding hardware, installing software, or changing a line of code.

Our customers range from Fortune 500 companies and nonprofits to small businesses and budding entrepreneurs. Every day, about 12,000 new customers sign up. We’re working to create a faster, more secure, and more reliable experience for anyone online and given the scale at which we operate, our mission is big. Our team is hard at work shaping the future of the Internet by solving some of its toughest challenges. Come join us.

 

About the Department

Cloudflare’s IT Team oversees and supports the internal hardware, software, and services offered to Cloudflare employees. This includes our internal corporate network, internal apps, management of our Windows, Mac, Linux, Android, and iOS devices, and our telecommunications systems, as well as onboarding and offboarding employees. We currently have IT Operations team members offering in-person support in San Francisco, London, and soon--Singapore.



Responsibilities

We are looking for a Head of IT to join our team. You will lead a multinational IT operations group comprised of teams in each of Cloudflare’s key countries. Your mission will be to support Cloudflare’s diverse team (no matter where they might be) and maintain the technology that supports them. You will gather and organize information to make data-driven decisions about long-term strategy and help the enterprise scale to meet its challenging mission.

You will also be in charge of increasing headcount for the IT Operations team as well as creating new IT teams as the company grows and needs arise (e.g. IT Networking, IT AV, IT Data storage, etc)

A crucial component of this position will be consolidating, maintaining, and supporting our internal apps, both on-premise and SaaS. Ensuring that we do not have redundancy in our internal app offerings, leading integrating with other apps and SSO where possible, working with providers to ensure uptime and good support.

Your data-driven decisions will be driving towards reducing unnecessary support requests, improving the support requests that remain, and anticipating the needs of Cloudflare users as the company moves forward, so that technology, processes, and documentation can be ready for changes. Also, making decisions regarding our internal app offerings, improving the quality and efficiency of our internal hardware, apps, networks, and services.

Cloudflare is a leading internet company with some of the top technology and employees in the world, and you’ll help our internal tech and services be as impressive as our external ones.

  • Lead the global IT team, growing it to meet business needs.
  • Help Cloudflare scale into the global enterprise that it needs to be.
  • Manage the applications offered to employees
  • Define IT Infrastructure strategy, architecture and procedures, optimizing functionality and cost
  • Be the champion for IT across the business - ensure all teams and departments have the resources they need.
  • Coaching, mentoring and advancing both the business and technical skills of team members

Requirements

  • 7+ years in IT management
  • Genuine passion for IT
  • Experience in leading a globally diverse team
  • Bachelor’s degree in information technology or equivalent experience.
  • Experience scaling IT within a rapidly growing enterprise environment.
  • Strong project management skills
  • Ability to appropriately delegate tasks and utilize expertise available to you
  • Demonstrate a track record of managing a team including hiring, on-boarding, and professional development. You inspire your team to reach higher. You’re as good as explaining “why” as you are “how”
  • Can-do, collaborative, client servicing mindset and ability to influence and operate effectively in a matrixed and global environment
  • Experience implementing tools, process, methodologies and resolving blockages
  • Well versed with IT fundamentals including Agile, IT investment /portfolio management, Enterprise Architecture, Information Risk Management, ITIL, and Service Management

Bonus Points

  • Master’s degree in Information Technology or related field
  • Familiarity with IT management systems and Enterprise IT applications such as Active Directory, Casper, MDMs
  • Security background and qualifications such as CISSP or CISM.

What Makes Us Special

We’re not just a highly ambitious, large-scale technology company. We’re a highly ambitious, large-scale technology company with a soul. Fundamental to our mission to help build a better Internet is protecting the free and open Internet. In 2014, we launched Project Galileo, an initiative through which we partner with global NGOs to identify websites at risk of attack and provide the same state-of-the-art mitigation technology already used by Cloudflare’s enterprise customers--at no cost. Project Galileo equips politically and artistically important organizations and journalists with powerful tools to defend themselves against attacks that would otherwise censor their work.

Additionally, in 2016, we announced our partnership with Path Forward, a nonprofit organization that works with companies to create 18-week positions for mid-career professionals who want to get back to the workplace after taking time off to care for a child, parent, or loved one. With the lofty goal of shaping the future of the Internet, we’re focused on recruiting the best and the brightest, no matter what.
