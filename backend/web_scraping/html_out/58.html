Description

As a SQL Analyst, you will play a critical role in driving the development and ongoing enhancement to key reporting infrastructure, tools and analytics that will enable RH Call Centers to optimize effective engagement with our customers. This team is small but plays a pivotal role in providing analytics to drive decision-making throughout RH Customer Support. As a member of the Workforce Planning team, you’ll have an opportunity to influence every decision we make.

You’ll help us understand the needs of our customers, assess the effectiveness of our current tools, and estimate the impact of key business decisions. 

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Help us improve our customer experience through analysis of workforce data trends
  • Gather data via SQL or OLAP Cube
  • Discover and recommend business opportunities through data analysis and design experiments to test them
  • Present your findings and insights to Call Center leadership and key stakeholders across our Supply Chain
  • Perform ad-hoc analyses to support optimization and company vision
  • Complete post-mortem analysis to highlight areas of opportunities and planning adjustments

 

REQUIREMENTS

  • Professional experience in call center, distribution center or retail environment, with thorough understanding of related metrics, processes and practices
  • Familiarity with Avaya phone data structure
  • Professional experience presenting complex results to non-technical audiences
  • Prior experience with Salesforce reporting
  • Advanced-level SQL knowledge, including SSRS and SSAS
  • Excel expertise, including advanced formulas, macros, and tables
  • Proficiency in scripting languages such as Ruby, Python, or JavaScript 
  • Schedule flexibility to see a project/analysis to completion, which may require nights and/or weekends
  • BS in Computer Science, MIS, or similar field of study preferred
