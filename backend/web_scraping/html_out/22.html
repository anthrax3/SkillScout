Description
We count on our host/hostess for a number of duties. The host/hosts is responsible for cheerfully greeting the guests, escort them to their table, provide them with a menu and answer any preliminary questions. Other duties include: monitoring table rotations and a keeping track of prepared tables for new guests, answering the phone, take reservations and in some cases managing take-out orders.

If you are looking for host, hostess, maitre d, matradee, maitres d please apply to this position
More
Requirements/Responsibilities
- 2 years of experience is preferred but we will train the right person also
- A warm and outgoing personality with a passion for food and customer service
- Experience with OpenTable is desirable
- Must have a commitment to deliver a unique and memorable guest experience
- Punctual, patient, and mindful
- Fluent in English and proficiency in other languages is a plus
- Familiarity with OpenTable is a plus
