Digital Marketing Manager

Marketing San Carlos, California

Apply

Description

Shutterfly is looking for a highly motivated, creative thinker with outstanding digital marketing and customer acquisition skills, including hands-on experience with display and search engine marketing (SEM). You will help strategize, implement and manage overall digital advertising, including organic search.  The right candidate will need to jump in to help drive traffic and awareness of the BorrowLenses brand, where professional photographers and videographers come to rent premium gear online.  

The Digital Marketing Manager will assist in growing BorrowLenses web presence, awareness and customer acquisition by planning, executing and optimizing our online marketing efforts and strategies.  This will include engaging in search engine optimization, search engine marketing, display marketing, web content and topic model authoring and other web marketing activities. This important role will include working cross functionally with internal teams, agencies, reporting and presenting content marketing plans and progress. 

Responsibilities include:

  • Customer acquisition efforts across multiple channels.
  • Optimize existing channels and test new channels to drive y/y new customer growth and AOS.
  • Identifying and recommending areas for growth, including via organic search, display, retargeting or other virtuous cyclical growth loops.
  • Brainstorming and developing relevant and high quality creative content designed to naturally attract links, grow organic traffic and drive awareness.
  • Working with the social media, CRM and website merchandising teams to distribute the content created, optimize website, blogs and driving relevant traffic.
  • Developing and implementing tests at a rapid pace.
  • Monitoring brand mentions and reputation.
  • Execution of weekly and monthly reporting across marketing programs, leveraging Google Analytics, Omniture, Conductor, etc.
  • Collaboration on demand generation plans, programs, and campaigns.

 

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in business, marketing, organizational development or related field, or equivalent experience.
  • 3-5 years of experience in web marketing with demonstrated depth in SEM and content marketing responsibilities. Experience with social media is a big plus.
  • Excellent copywriting skills and on-brand analysis
  • Detail-oriented and an excellent problem-solver. Can multi-task and juggle many simultaneous priorities.
  • Excellent project management skills and understanding of brand and content marketing best practices.
  • An understanding of the search engine marketing industry, paid search, display and retargeting, and best practices across these channels.
  • Experience with Google Analytics is a must. Experience with Google AdWords, Moz, Conductor, BrightEdge, and other analytics, PPC and SEO tools is a big plus.
  • Knowledge of HTML and content management systems.
  • Proficient in standard Microsoft, Adobe design programs.
  • Culture fit is important. Familiarity with photography or videography is a plus.

 

*Working at Shutterfly*

Our employees at Shutterfly are passionate and possess the deeply held belief that a team working for a cause will reach and benefit millions of people.  We are looking for those rare individuals who share our passion about building a great company while delighting our customers, partners and fellow employees.

We keep a fun and casual environment at Shutterfly. Free drinks and snacks are always available, and free gear rental is a unique perk.  We have the excitement of the dotcom era and the stability of an established, profitable company.

We are easy to find off 101 in San Carlos.
