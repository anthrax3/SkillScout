Technical Project Manager

at Marqeta (View all jobs)
Oakland, California

The Role
We are seeking a talented and unique individual to help us manage and deliver key technical projects in a fast-paced, high-growth, start-up environment. This role requires a diverse and high level technical knowledge set, stamina, quick thinking, an ability to easily context switch, and the ability to make tough decisions. You will have the opportunity to focus on projects across numerous technical fronts, including regular platform releases and new data center builds with complex network requirements across many partners. In addition, you will be working and coordinating with large third-party vendors and service providers. The nature of the role and project domain will require off-hours work on occasion. This is a highly visible role with tremendous opportunity to learn and grow.

What you'll do
- Engage and coordinate with a diverse set of teams,both internal and external, on a daily basis
- Plan and execute complex and often long-term technical projects
- Manage smaller technical efforts, periodically executed after business hours
- Coordinate new program build-outs from the DevOps angle
- Assist with monthly software release management
- Schedule weekly deployment calendar
- Utilize Jenkins for branch and build creation
- Triage JIRA issues and follow though until completion

Who we are looking for
- Experience in project management in addition to a background in either development, quality assurance, or another technical related endeavor.
- Experience in release management utilizing GitHub + Jenkins.
- Administration level skill set in Atlassian Cloud + JIRA + Confluence
- Strong communication skills and a direct style
- Inherent organizational skills
- Ability to multitask and prioritize complicated projects
- Ability to garner strong working relationships across multiple teams is a must
- Local candidates only, relocation costs not being offered

Bonus points for
- Experience in the financial industry
- MySQL user experience
- Python skills
- Linux skills
- Good sense of humor
- Aversion to Gantt charts

- Competitive salary and benefits package

- Great location in a beautiful new office in downtown Oakland
- Close to public transportation

- and so much more...
