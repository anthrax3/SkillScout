About Ethos Lending

Ethos Lending is an innovative mortgage lending company founded in 2013.  We are disrupting the mortgage industry through our technology and state of the art platform.  We continuously reduce the cost of a mortgage loan and deliver the most transparent, efficient, simple, and consistent ecosystem and experience to its three primary stakeholders – team members, consumers, and mortgage brokers.  The company’s passionate and empathetic culture helps everyone in the loan process succeed.  Since our founding in 2013, we have originated over $4 billion in mortgage loans.  Our goal is to fundamentally transform the mortgage industry.   

We are a team of builders, disrupters, and innovators. We are rapidly growing and expanding and are looking for those who have a great desire to work hard and succeed with high growth opportunities.  Our core values are to be Responsive, Proactive, Accountable, Transparent, Efficient, and Ethical. If this excites you, read on. 


The Role

You will be integral to our success by driving our production in Sales and allowing us to be the #1 wholesale lender in the nation. An Inside Sales Specialist at Ethos is ambitious, competitive, and loves to sell. You’ll be a successful sales leader with entrepreneurial mindset who is ready to take on all challenges. At Ethos, you’ll be representing the next generation of mortgage lending and creating strategic ideas for our continuous growth. 

Responsibilities

  • Achieve and exceed monthly and quarterly goals
  • Achieve new broker approvals, funding and forward lock goals
  • Source new sales opportunities through high-volume inbound lead follow-up, outbound calls, and emails
  • Research accounts, identify key leads, and close the deal
  • Maintain and expand CRM with prospects and approved accounts within assigned territories
  • Train and educate accounts on Ethos proprietary systems, CRM, Encompass, Admin Portal, programs, products, guidelines and processes
  • Build and nurture long-term relationships and partnerships with your accounts, which includes continuous interaction with brokers to keep them updated on existing and upcoming programs or changes
  • Manage loan pipeline and monitor the performance of accounts
  • Maintain sales contact, pipeline, and other pertinent sales activity reports
  • Remain current on company requirements and ensure compliance
  • Perform effective online training to potential prospects and new accounts

Qualifications
  • College degree is desired
  • Thrives in a fast-paced, fluid start-up environment
  • Demonstrated ability to maintain a high level of achievement
  • Self-starter able to excel in a team-oriented environment while working independently on individual goals and metrics
  • Excellent written, oral, and influencing skills
  • Obsessed about the customer experience
  • Innovative attitude dedicated to success
