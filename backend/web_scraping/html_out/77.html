Job Description

Urbano Latino in Oakland is looking to hire for Bartender position.

We are a one year old restaurant that is growing and getting busier.

Bartender Requirements:

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES include the following. Perform, Serving/bar tending experience: 

-minimum 2 year of experience in a high volume establishment.

-Knowledge of wine and spirits a must.

-Fast learner and outgoing personality. Able to provide suggestions to guests and make proper recommendations for pairing. 

-Reliable transportation

-Must be available to work both weekends & holiday's.

-NEED to be able to be flexible in roles. a couple of shifts as server. If performing well, will increase shifts as server. 

-Must be able to learn menu and wine list and be comfortable with related terminology

-able to multitask and keep calm under pressure. Able to keep up with high volume and fast pace bar scene. Orientation to detail and organized. 

-POS system experience . . .Ability to describe and suggest menu features.

-Overseeing table maintenance.

-Maintaining the flow of service throughout the dining room.

-Performs any necessary pre-shift side work. *

-Observe all safety procedures. Maintain an organized and clean work area at all times. 

-flexible scheduling. Able to cover shifts when needed in addition to existing shifts given.

-Sets up or restocks his or her work area, equipment, utensils.... Keeps the work area clean throughout the shift.

BEFORE YOU REPLY, READ THIS!

Please reply via email with your resume copied into the email field and detailing relevant experience.

Make sure your contact phone number and email are clearly noted in your reply.

ATTACHMENTS WILL NOT BE OPENED

About Urbano Latino

Urbano Latino is a fun restaurant specialized in latin food starting with Chicken Mole, Tacos, Enchiladas, ending with Paella. We have an interesting and fun cocktail list
